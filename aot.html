What I do

Hands-on organisation from concept to microphone check.

Programme & speaker care

  • Recruit and brief early-career and senior researchers for 15–20 minute bilingual talks.
  • Workshop slides for accessibility: minimal jargon, strong visuals, clear story arc.
  • Facilitate audience Q&A and post-talk discussions to keep conversations flowing.

Production & promotion

  • Secure venues, manage AV, and coordinate volunteers for ticketing and crowd flow.
  • Design monthly visuals and announcements across Instagram and partner mailing lists.
  • Record and publish talks to YouTube for reach beyond Paris.
