Research themes

Interlocking questions driving my work

Method-heavy astrophysics that links survey data to cosmological insight.

Weak-lensing mass estimation

Designing filters and likelihoods that stabilise shear measurements, deliver robust cluster mass calibrations, and scale to next-generation data from Euclid and LSST.

Cluster abundance cosmology

Building likelihoods that include super-sample covariance, tuning halo mass function calibrations, and exploring simulation-based inference for cleaner cosmological constraints.

Cross-correlations & gravity tests

Linking galaxies, CMB lensing, and radio dipoles to probe f(R) gravity, intrinsic alignments, and moving-lens effects across multiple wavelengths.

Survey leadership & community

Active in Euclid’s cluster science working groups, organiser of outreach series Astronomy on Tap Paris, and reviewer for AAS journals.
